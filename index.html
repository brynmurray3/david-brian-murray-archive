David Murray

March 22, 1960 – July 21, 2024

This is a family archive created to preserve the memories, videos, photographs, and stories of David Murray — known to everyone who loved him as Dad, friend, and the kind of person who made wherever he was feel like the right place to be.

He was a cyclist, a thinker, and someone whose warmth was felt by everyone around him. He is deeply loved, and he is deeply missed.
