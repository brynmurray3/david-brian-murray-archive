David Murray

March 22, 1960 – July 21, 2024

Welcome

This archive was created to preserve the life and memories of David Brian Murray — my dad.

He was a cyclist, a professor, a friend, and the kind of person who made wherever he was feel like the right place to be. My dad had a rare way of connecting with people. He remembered the details of your life, listened when you spoke, and made people feel genuinely seen.

More than anything, he was loved. And he still is.

This website also serves as a place to access and preserve the videos from his Muzz America Tour — the cross-country ride where he documented moments of gratitude, reflection, and connection along the road.

Use the navigation above to explore the videos, browse photographs, and read memories from the people who knew and loved him.
